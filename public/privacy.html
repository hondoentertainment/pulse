Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Location Data

Pulse requires location access (GPS) to determine your proximity to venues and verify your Check-Ins. Your precise location is never shared publicly. Venues only aggregate anonymized "Energy Scores" from proximate users.

2. Content Moderation

Any content (Pulses) containing hate speech, harassment, fake location spoofing, or illegal material will be deleted. Accounts bridging these terms will be terminated without prior notice.

3. Data Retention

Historical check-in data is retained only to calculate your "Pioneer" status and credibility score. Routine telemetry is deleted after 30 days.

4. Third Parties

We share anonymous usage data with Vercel and Sentry purely for performance and crash diagnostics. We do not sell your data to any third-party marketing firms.
