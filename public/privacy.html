Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy describes how Pulse collects, uses, and deletes personal data. It applies to the Pulse web app and related services.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account: email, username, profile photo, and preferences you set in Settings.
  • Location: GPS coordinates to verify check-ins and show nearby venues. Precise location is not shown publicly.
  • Content: pulses (photos, videos, captions, energy ratings), reactions, and follows.
  • Device: push notification tokens (if enabled), crash/performance diagnostics.
  • Safety Kit (optional): emergency contacts and safety session metadata when you enable that feature.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate venue discovery, scoring, social feeds, and notifications.
  • Prevent abuse via moderation, rate limits, and credibility weighting.
  • Improve reliability (error monitoring, performance metrics).

We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

3. Location Data

Pulse requires location access (GPS) to determine your proximity to venues and verify check-ins. Your precise location is never shared publicly. Venues only receive aggregated, anonymized energy signals from proximate users.

4. Sharing With Service Providers

We use trusted processors to run Pulse:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting.
  • Vercel — application hosting and serverless APIs.
  • Sentry — crash and error diagnostics (when configured).

Each provider is bound by their own privacy terms and our data-processing agreements where applicable.

5. Data Retention

  • Pulses expire from live feeds after ~90 minutes; database copies may persist for moderation and analytics windows.
  • Check-in history supports credibility badges and is retained while your account is active.
  • Diagnostic telemetry is typically retained up to 30 days.
  • Safety Kit session logs follow the retention schedule in product documentation (generally up to 2 years for audit trails).

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access / export — Settings → Export My Data downloads a JSON bundle of your account data.
  • Delete — Settings → Delete Account permanently removes your profile and auth record (cascading related data).
  • Correct — update profile fields in the app.
  • Opt out — disable notifications, presence, or optional features in Settings.

For requests we cannot fulfill in-app, contact support with your account email.

7. Content Moderation

Content containing hate speech, harassment, fake location spoofing, or illegal material may be removed. Accounts that repeatedly violate our Terms of Service may be terminated.

8. Children

Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other regions where our infrastructure providers operate. We apply appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers where required.

10. Changes

We will update this policy when practices change. Check the “Last updated” date above.
